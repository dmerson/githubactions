About digitalArizona

digitalArizona is a software solutions company built by Arizonans, for Arizona. We partner with businesses, nonprofits, and communities across the state to design and deliver technology that drives real impact.

Our Mission

To accelerate Arizona's digital future by building software solutions that are practical, scalable, and rooted in the needs of our local communities.

Our Story

Founded in Phoenix, digitalArizona grew out of a simple observation: too many local organizations were being left behind by rapidly changing technology. Our founders set out to change that — creating a firm that combines enterprise-grade engineering with genuine community investment.

Today we serve clients from Tucson to Flagstaff, delivering web platforms, mobile applications, data integrations, and custom software tailored to each organization's unique goals.

Our Values

  • Local FirstArizona is our home. We prioritize partnerships that strengthen the Arizona economy and community.
  • TransparencyNo hidden fees, no black-box processes. You always know what we're building and why.
  • QualityWe ship software we're proud to put our name on — tested, documented, and built to last.
  • AccessibilityDigital solutions should work for everyone. We build with inclusion as a core requirement, not an afterthought.
